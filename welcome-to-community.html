Welcome to the community.

Thank you for joining the OHS Software Foundation community. We're excited to collaborate with you on building the foundation for global digital health.

⚠️ One quick step: whitelist us

Because institutional, university and hospital firewalls have incredibly strict spam filters, our invites can sometimes get lost. To make sure you receive our newsletter and calendar links:

  1. Open your email client.
  2. Add hello@ohs.foundation to your address book or safe-senders list.
  3. If you find our first email in your “Promotions” or “Spam” folder, drag it to your primary inbox.

🗓️ Step 2: Save the community calls

What to expect next
